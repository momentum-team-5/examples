Bears

Bear Facts

  • Their scientific name is "Ursidae."
  • The Polar Bear can reach speeds of up to 25 mph.
  • The Giant Panda has a 20 year life span.
  • Sizes of some bears:
    • 150-220 lbs (Giant panda)
    • 990 lbs (Polar bear)
    • 240 lbs (American black bear)
    • 180-1,300 lbs (Brown bear)
